Prismatica Magazine's Comment Policy

Since "don't be a dick" isn't, well, a very good guideline on how to comment, Prismatica Magazine has put together a comment policy for which to comply.

Please refrain from the following:

  • Personal attacks against another commenter or the writer of the post/website. This can run from name calling to sentences which include the words “people like you” or statements which begin “I bet you” or “you must/probably” etc.
  • Hate speech or other clearly intentional jerk behavior. This includes racist, gendered, ableist, homophobic and transphobic slurs and language of any sort.
  • Every interpretation of spamming is prohibited. This means no unauthorized advertisements, links to any kind of online gambling, game currency sellers, follower sellers, self-promotion of mobile games, YouTube channels or unsanctioned crowdfunding, and charity efforts** are to be posted in the comments.
