Editor's Desk: August

As we enter September and the second month of Prismatica Magazine, I would like to thank you all for the loving support we have recieved since starting up this little adventure with you all.

With more LGBTQ+ writers and artists becoming aware of our magazine, we hope to see an increase in submissions and, as a friendly reminder, we are hosting a Free Edit Week for the first seven days of September. Finally, if you like what you've seen and read from our magazine please consider our Patreon; we love running Prismatica Magazine and only want to improve our service too you all, supporting us will help us pay our Writers and Staff, as well as expand our services.

With felt gratitude, Matthew Gustavson

Free Edit Week

Hey there! Have a piece of fiction that you think just isnâ€™t quite ready to be published? For one week only, from September 1st through the 7th, the editing staff will edit any and all submissions that are sent in! Make sure you let us know in the email that the submission is for our Editing Week.

An editor will work directly with you, the author, on big picture stuff (developmental editing) where we help you build up your plot, work on characters, or anything else you want. Let us know if there is anything in particular you want us to work with you on, otherwise we will do a more generic development edit. Additionally, we will work on smaller things like line editing, to include sentence structure and grammatical errors.

This may take time, depending on how many submissions we receive, but we will edit anything sent to us that does not violate our submission guidelines. We will be working on submissions in the order that they are received and we will email you with updates along the way. Send in submissions as you would one to be published, indicating that it is for editing week as well as what type of editing you would like to be done (you can even choose both!).

We look forward to seeing your work!
