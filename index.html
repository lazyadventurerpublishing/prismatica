Editor's Desk: August

We are heading into the month of August, which means that we've been running for a little over one month now. I honestly can't believe how far we've come since developing the concept of the magazine in May, to estabishing the website in late-June, to pushing out our first story.

Collective Realms Magazine is currently two stories in with two more currently in development. We are going to continue on the set track of posting every two weeks, so keep a look out for the stories to come. The members of the Collective are a talented bunch who really deserve a lot of recognition. If you can take the time, please leave comments letting them know what you think. They put a lot of hard work into writing their stories, and I believe it shows.

I'd like to end with thanking all of our readers for being the encouragement we need in developing the magazine. I also want to personally thank my editing staff for their hard work in getting these stories published, and our podcasting staff in establishing our podcast. I am truly honored to be working among so many talented individuals. Thank you for letting me be your co-editor-in-chief.

-Andrew Olvera
