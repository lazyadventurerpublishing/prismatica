Editor's Desk: August

As we enter September and the second month of Prismatica Magazine, I would like to thank you all for the loving support we have recieved since starting up this little adventure with you all.

With more LGBTQ+ writers and artists becoming aware of our magazine, we hope to see an increase in submissions and, as a friendly reminder, we are hosting a Free Edit Week for the first seven days of September. Finally, if you like what you've seen and read from our magazine please consider our Patreon; we love running Prismatica Magazine and only want to improve our service too you all, supporting us will help us pay our Writers and Staff, as well as expand our services.

With felt gratitude, Matthew Gustavson
