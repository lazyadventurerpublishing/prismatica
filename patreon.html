We publish every issue of Prismatica online for free. We count on our readers and listeners to help support our efforts. Through Patreon, you can do just that:

What will we do with your contributions?

  • Be able to pay those writers for their work! We would love to be able to pay our writers for their work and with your support, we can do so.
  • Maintain our website and podcast, which features our fiction in audio format.
  • Create an e'zine that can be delivered electronically so that our content can be accessed away from your browser.

Our staff works entirely on a voluntary basis. Any contributions will be put directly back into the magazine itself to continue creating Prismatica, to include finding, publishing, and housing quality LGBTQ fiction for everyone.
