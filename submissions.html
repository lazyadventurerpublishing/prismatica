Prismatica Magazine is looking for character-oriented stories. While Prismatica Magazine does not immediately deny stories with adult themes, works with excessive explicit material will need to be edited to the satisfaction of our editorial staff.

Stories should be well-written, easily read on-screen, and effective in audio format. Genres that lie anywhere within the confines of fantasy or science-fiction are accepted, even “light” genres such as contemporary or urban fantasy with low-magic settings.

All submissions must be from self-identified LGBTQ+ members, though some exceptions are made on a case-by-case basis. If your story does not feature characters that identify somewhere on the LGBTQ+ spectrum, your story likely isn’t for us. This includes instances of “oh, they were gay the whole time, it just didn’t come up”. Stories from diverse perspectives and under-represented groups, settings, and cultures are gladly accepted, as are stories that discuss political and social issues.

We do not accept stories over 10,000 words or partial/incomplete stories. We do not feature serialized fiction, nor will we ask for the rest of the piece if it is not submitted in its entirety. Stories we likely will not accept include those about abusers or murderers (written as the protagonist), stories that are clearly about your Dungeons and Dragons campaign, or stories that paint any religion as the bad guy for edginess’s sake.

Fiction Guidelines

Word Limit: 500 - 10,000 words

Genres: Fantasy and science fiction

Rights: Upon acceptance of your work, Prismatica claims Nonexclusive Archival Internet Rights to showcase your work indefinitely (text and audio). We gladly accept work that has been printed elsewhere and do not claim First Rights. By submitting your work to prismatica.mag@gmail.com, you are agreeing to these terms. Additionally:

  • The editorial team reserves the right to accept or reject any article submitted for publication.
  • The editorial team reserves the right to edit articles for typographical, spelling, grammatical errors, and conformity with selected style guides.
  • Any other edits will be requested of the author and agreed upon between the author and editor prior to preparation for publication.

Fiction Submissions Process

Submit all stories to prismatica.mag@gmail.com. You should receive an email from us with 24 hours to confirm your submission. Our general response time is within 7 days.

Be sure to include your name, email address, story title, word count, genre and story. Please submit stories in standard manuscript format and in .RTF, .DOC, or .DOCX format.

Confidentiality

All personal information will never be shared with anyone outside of the editorial staff, except:

  • if compelled by law
  • if threats are made against staff
  • if narrators need assistance with pronunciation (email only)
  • if instructed by the author

Poetry Guidelines

In addition to the general content, genre, and rights guidelines for fiction please take in to consideration the following when submitting poetry:

  • Original works written in the English language as well as translations in to English will be considered.
  • Poems in both metered and non-metered styles are accepted. Additionally, there are no restrictions on poetry form.
  • Poetry submissions should not exceed 1,000 words.
  • Poems should be sent in single spaced with the exception of those pieces that make use of stylistic spacing for visual impact.

Art Guidelines

Prismatica Magazine features artwork as the cover of the month. Our rights are restricted to the cover or in the online archive of previous artwork. All other rights remain with the artist. Artists' bio and links to their gallery will be posted to our website.

Art Submissions Process

Art submissions must be 8.5 x 11 inches at a minimum of 72 dpi. Traditional artwork must be 300dpi or greater. Please submit artwork in .png, .tiff, .pdf, or .psds file types. Submit all artwork to prismatica.mag@gmail.com. You should receive an email from us with 24 hours to confirm your submission. Our general response time is within 7 days.

Be sure to include your name, email address, and piece title.
